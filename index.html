    Is it really 'YOUR' desire?

        We spend every moment by 'seeing' something by others, and then buy things, thereby satisfying our own desires. The desire to express self-identity (Desire to Exist), the desire to be recognized by others (Desire to Approval), and the desire to own something (Desire to Own) are all formed through our own experiences and memories, and these 3 desires are repeated as a direct cause of another consumption.

        Through this website, viewers can have time to reflect on their desires and meanings for consumption after exploring various cases.
